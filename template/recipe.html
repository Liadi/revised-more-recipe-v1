Eba

Ingredients

Item Quantity
Salt 1 pinch
Water 50 litres
Garri 2 kg

Instruction

  1. Boil the garri
  2. Pour the water into the Garri
  3. Seive shaft and dirt afloat
  4. Cover and wait for about 24 hours
  5. Stir vigourously
  6. Put in wraps
  7. Serve with fried water
